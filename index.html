Frequently Asked Questions

expand_more How do I register as a voter?
You can register online at voters.eci.gov.in using Form 6, or visit your nearest Electoral Registration Office with your ID proof and address proof. You must be at least 18 years old on the qualifying date.
expand_more Can I vote without an Aadhaar Card?
Yes! Aadhaar is not mandatory for voting. Your Voter ID (EPIC) is the primary document. If you don't have a Voter ID, you can use other approved photo IDs like a Passport or Driving License if your name is on the electoral roll.
expand_more What is NOTA?
NOTA stands for "None Of The Above". It allows voters to officially reject all candidates. It's the last option on the EVM and ensures your right to express dissent while maintaining voter secrecy.
